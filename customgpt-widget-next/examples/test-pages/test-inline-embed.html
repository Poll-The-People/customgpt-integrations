About Our Voice Assistant

Our AI-powered voice assistant brings natural conversation to your website. Using cutting-edge speech recognition and synthesis, it provides an intuitive way for your users to interact with information.

The assistant is embedded directly in your page content, making it a seamless part of your user experience rather than a separate popup or modal.

Integration Benefits

By embedding the voice assistant inline, you create a more integrated experience for your users. They can scroll to the assistant naturally as part of your content, rather than having to find and click a floating button.

This approach is perfect for:

  • FAQ sections where users want quick voice answers
  • Product pages where voice search is valuable
  • Support pages where conversational help is needed
  • Landing pages with voice-enabled CTAs

How It Works

The integration is incredibly simple. Just add an iframe with your deployment URL:

<!-- Add container where you want the assistant --> <div style="width: 100%; height: 600px;"> <iframe src="https://your-project.vercel.app" width="100%" height="100%" frameborder="0" allow="microphone" style="border-radius: 8px; box-shadow: 0 4px 6px rgba(0,0,0,0.1);"> </iframe> </div>

Deployment URLs

After deploying to Vercel or Railway, replace the iframe src with your actual deployment:

  • Vercel: https://your-project.vercel.app
  • Railway: https://your-project.railway.app
  • Custom Domain: https://yourdomain.com

Important: HTTPS is required for microphone access in production.
