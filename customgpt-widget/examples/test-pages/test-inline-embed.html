Integration Benefits

By embedding the voice assistant inline, you create a more integrated experience for your users. They can scroll to the assistant naturally as part of your content, rather than having to find and click a floating button.

This approach is perfect for:

  • FAQ sections where users want quick voice answers
  • Product pages where voice search is valuable
  • Support pages where conversational help is needed
  • Landing pages with voice-enabled CTAs

How It Works

The integration is incredibly simple. Just add a container div and include our script: 

<!-- Add container where you want the assistant -->
<div id="customgpt-widget-embed"></div>

<!-- Load the script -->
<script src="../embed-scripts/script-inline-embed.js"></script>
